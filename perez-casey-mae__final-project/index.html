Our goal is to bring more perspective into story-driven video games. We wish to explore the point of view of more than just the protagonist of the story, allowing for a more thorough understanding of human nature. We believe that people are inherently morally gray, so we seek to embrace diverse viewpoints in each and every one of our stories.
